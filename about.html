About Geniqe

We are a team of professionals from Retail Industry having more than 20 years of experience in retail market trend in food service & food retail industry. We are specialized in offering turnkey solution for Food Retail Chains Setups by providing Refrigeration equipment’s, application related design & execution of Cold Rooms, Frozen Rooms & Store Fixtures.

Along with equipment & fixture sales we are well versed in taking service contracts for maintenance and also retrofitting your existing equipment setup with different applications for aesthetics, quality performance & energy efficiency.
